Dog Food

Description

I have this dog named Stella. One time I got up to feed her and noticed that

her bag of food was getting empty. I didn't care because I have the best recipe

for dog food ever. Stella would rather eat this meal then that bagged crap any day.

prep time- 5 minutes

cook time- 15 minutes

total time- 20 minutes

servings- 2 servings

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup of Minute Rice(TM)
  • 1/2 cup of water
  • Bag of frozen corn or peas
  • 1 block of marble cheese
  • Dried liver bited
  • A pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Add 1/2 cup of water to a small pot and add your pinch of salt.
  2. Boil water until it reaches a rolling boil and add your 1/2 cup of Minute Rice(TM).
  3. Put a lid on the pot and turn stove top off. The rice will be ready in 10 minutes
  4. In the meantime add 1/4 of frozen peas to a bowl.
  5. Slice 2 slices of chesese and cut them into bite size pieces.
  6. Take your dried liver bites, crush them up and add them to the bowl.
  7. Once the rice is done, transfer it to a dish and add your other bowl of peas, cheese and liver.
  8. Let cool and serve warm.
