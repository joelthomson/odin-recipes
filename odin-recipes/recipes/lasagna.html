Lasagna

Desription

Imagine it's a cool Autumn evening. Your mother had just gone to Costco the day before and bought

a frozen lasagna. And she said she was going to make lasagna tonight. Well here is how to make it.

prep time- 30 seconds

cook time- 1 hour

total time- 1 hour and 30 seconds

servings- like 2 plates at dinner and then you take lasgana lunge to work the next day

Ingredients

  • 1 Costco membership
  • 1 car to drive to Costco
  • Legs to walk to the frozen aisle in Costco*
  • 1 Cleebee to accompny you
*If you do not possess a legs you are more then welcome to use those stupid motorized wheelchairs they provide.

Steps

  1. Drive to Costco. Don't forget your membership.
  2. Use your legs or those stuuuupid fucking motor chairs and go to the frozen aisle.
  3. Grab your frozen lasagna and go home.
  4. When you get home pre-heat your oven to 375 degrees celcius.
  5. Once pre-heated place lasagna on oven rack.
  6. Bake for 1 hour.
  7. Remove from oven, allow 10 minutes to cool and then serve.
